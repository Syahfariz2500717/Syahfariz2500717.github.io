The Story Behind This Project

During my second year Psychology Research Methods course, I conducted an independent study exploring how lifestyle factors affect academic outcomes. As a student juggling coursework, part-time work, and extracurricular activities, I noticed my own grades fluctuated with my sleep schedule. This sparked my curiosity: Was there a quantifiable relationship between sleep and academic performance across the student population?

Research Context:

  • Timeline: Conducted over Fall 2023 semester
  • Sample: 85 undergraduate students (voluntary participation)
  • Method: Daily sleep logs + end-of-semester GPA collection
  • Ethics: Anonymous data collection with institutional approval
  • Personal Motivation: To make data-driven decisions about my own study habits
